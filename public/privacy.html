Privacy notice

Labrador Critical Minerals Explorer · last updated 2026-07-14

This is a free public map of Labrador critical-mineral context built from government open data. We collect as little as possible.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics (privacy-oriented, no advertising cookies) to count page views and a small set of product events (for example: layer toggles, export, feedback). Plausible does not use cross-site tracking cookies.

Local storage on your device

The app may store on this device only (not cookies for ads):

  • Whether you dismissed the welcome tips
  • Your last map view (layers, position, filters) so return visits resume where you left off
  • Optional KPI display preferences

You can clear this via your browser’s site data controls.

Email forms (Stay updated / Feedback)

If you submit an email or message, it is delivered to the project operator through a form processor (Formspree and/or FormSubmit) or, as a fallback, your device’s email app (mailto). We use submissions to improve the map and, if you opted in, to send occasional product/data updates. We do not sell your email.

Third-party map tiles

Basemap imagery/tiles are loaded from providers such as CARTO, Esri, or OpenStreetMap-derived services as shown in the map attribution. Those providers may receive standard web request metadata (IP, user agent).

Contact

Questions about this notice: use Settings → Feedback in the app, or open an issue on the project repository.
